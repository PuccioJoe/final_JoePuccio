Should Alexander Mogilny be a Member of the Hockey Hall of Fame?

Over his NHL career, Alexander Mogilny racked up multiple individual awards including a Stanley Cup in 2000. His career in North America might've never come to fruition without external factors. Alexander Mogilny has been examined for years as the Hockey Hall of Fame in Toronto admits new members every year. This page takes a look at Mogilny's accomplishments on and off the ice.

  • 2000 Stanley Cup Champion
  • 6x All-Star
  • 2003 Lady Byng Trophy winner
  • Buffalo Sabres Hall of Famer
  • Greater Buffalo Sports Hall of Famer
